what is cyber security

Cyber security is the protection of systems and networks
for malicious attacks, whether that be damage or theft of information.

dangers of cyber threats

Cyber threats are in a never ending battle with cyber security.
When one gets stronger, the other will find ways to get around it. This is why it is so important to make sure you're knowledge on how to deal with cyber threats is comprehensive and up to date.

Specific risk to students

Although student's today grew up with technology, this does not make them
exempt from general and specific cyber threats that can face them. This website can help you in equipping yourself with an understanding of cyber security to protect yourself.
